About Us

The secret of success is in strict quality control of transportation and openness to a new idea! Ice cream and desserts Ice cream takes into account taste sensations around the world for a reason.

The recipe includes cream, fruits, berries, nuts, textures. Artificial additives, palm oil and other questionable components are excluded. The factories use an old recipe - you don't have to think about your health.

Ice Cream Company is the American leader in the variety of ice cream varieties. The Ice Cream palette has over 1000 flavors! Here are just a few of them: strawberry; raisin; cream biscuits; caramel; cherry; lemon with custard; chocolate chips; tiramisu; melon; rum; blueberries and more.

The Ice Cream ball is really big, unlike other firms. Special equipment for rolling ice cream (Ice cream specially trains its employees),the balls are larger than the standard size and weigh 70g, usually instead of 50g. Buy Ice Cream in your city stores or go to Ice Cream branded cafes in Chicago, Los Angeles and New York!
